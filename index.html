The World's Value Is Being Tokenized

The inception of Smart Contracts has enabled the creation of tokenized assets and buisness logics in immutable open networks. Hundreds of new businesses have been created on this new decentralized medium with more being added every moment.

Making Smart Contracts Simple

The process of development and execution of smart contracts is often complex, time-consuming, and error-prone. ContractLand aims drive the effort of mass ultilization of smart contracts by reducing technical barriers and grounding blockchain technology into real business use cases.

The ContractLand Platform

A decentralized ecosystem built by developers, businesses, and end-users

Developer DAO

Developers are incentivized by token rewards to participate in the creation of industry standard smart contract services

Smart Contract as a Service

Enterprises can browse and choose service that best fit their business needs, and deploy them using their own parameters

Decentralized Exchange

Assets created by businesses can be fairly and safely traded on ContractLand’s Decentralized Exchange, enabling instant liquidity and market exposure.

Diversified Smart Contract Services

Create a wide-range selection for companies in different industries

Tokens

In the future, most digital assets and goods will be tokenized. ContractLand provides highly-audited and standardlized smart contract for token creation at low cost

Token Sale

Token is one of the most prominent use cases of smart contract. Enterprises can use ContractLand’s secure token sale contracts to facilitate their own token issuing event

Factoring Contract

The factoring industry requires a large amount of manpower to identify the authenticity of traditional paper contracts. ContractLand’s digital factoring contracts enable higher liquidity with easier creation and verification

Pre-Paid Services

Utilizing smart contracts, ContractLand eliminates chargeback, central billing system failure or man-made destruction of the pre-paid industry, resulting in more efficient services

Event & Ticket Industry

ContractLand provides a transparent ticketing ecosystem on the blockchain, eliminating ticket fraud in the second-hand ticket market
