Built on Terra Technology

Terra-Chain is a next generation blockchain built for scalable and interoperable transactions across an internet of blockchain ecosystem.

Terra-Bridge is a bi-directional bridging technology for value transfers among different blockchain networks.

With Terra-Bridge, users can transfer their tokens from existing public chains such as Ethereum onto Terra-Chain, where users can exchange tokens directly from their address via the on-chain exchange system.
