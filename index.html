The World's Value Is Moving On-Chain

Blockchain technology enabled the creation of tokenized assets that are fungible, transferable, and verifiable. The inception of Smart Contracts extends these properties to complexed buisness logics. Hundreds of different business logics have been created on this new decentralized medium with more being added every moment. Blockchain is quickly assimulating into real world business operations.

Reducing Technical Barrier and Increasing Liquidity to Smart Contracts

Many small- and medium-sized enterprices have yet to benefit from blockchain’s nature of low-cost market penetration and high liquidity due to high technical barriers. ContractLand allows developers to create and share smart contract templates for various business logics, which enterprices could utilize to optimize their business process.

The ContractLand Ecosystem

A decentralized ecosystem built upon developers, enterprises, and users

Template Creation

Developers are incentivized by token rewards to participate in the creation of industry standard smart contract templates

Contract Deployment

Enterprises can browse and choose templates that best fit their business needs, and deploy them using their own parameters

Decentralized Exchange

Deployed contracts can be fairly and safely traded on ContractLand’s Decentralized Exchange, enabling both B2B and B2C transactions.

Diversified Templates

Create a wide-range selection of templates for companies in different industries

Tokens

In the future, most digital assets and goods will be tokenized. ContractLand provides highly-audited contract templates for token creation at low cost

Token Sale

Token is one of the most prominent use cases of smart contract. Enterprises can use ContractLand’s template to facilitate their own token sales

Factoring Contract

The factoring industry requires a large amount of manpower to identify the authenticity of traditional paper contracts. ContractLand’s factoring contract templates enable higher liquidity with easier creation and verification

Pre-Paid Services

Utilizing smart contracts, ContractLand eliminates chargeback, central billing system failure or man-made destruction of the pre-paid industry, resulting in more efficient services

Event & Ticket Industry

ContractLand provides a transparent ticketing ecosystem on the blockchain, eliminating ticket fraud in the second-hand ticket market
