The Liquidity Infrastructure of Web3.0

ContractLand builts the infrastructure to provide liquidity of tokenized assets across various blockchains through decentralized cross-chain bridging technology.

Benefits of ContractLand

Manage Assets

Securely manage assets across multiple blockchains with a single address that you control.

Exchange Tokens

Exchange tokens across chains directly from you address. No centralized third party will hold your tokens.

Build an Exchange

Integrate with our chain using existing Ethereum development tools to create an exchange, and get liquidity right when you launch with shared on-chain order-book.

Our Technology

Terra-Chain is a public blockchain built for scalable and interoperable transactions across an internet of blockchain ecosystem.

Terra-Bridge is a bi-directional bridging technology for value transfers among different blockchain networks.

With Terra-Bridge, users can transfer their tokens from existing public chains such as Ethereum onto Terra-Chain, where users can exchange tokens directly from their address via the on-chain exchange system.
