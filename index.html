The World's Value Moving On-Chain

Blockchain technology enabled the creation of tokenized assets that are fungiable, transferable, and verifiable. The inception of Smart Contracts extend these properties to complexed buisness logics. Hundreds of contracts have been created on this new decentralized medium with more being added every moment. Blockchain based assets are no longer just a topic for investors and technical experimentalists, but quickly assimulating into real world business operations.

Reducing Tehcnical Barrier and Increasing Liquidity to Smart Contracts

As an important part of the global economy, small and medium-sized enterprises have not enjoyed the innovations brought by blockchain, and it's low-cost market penetration and high liquidity. ContractLand allows developers to create and share business smart contract templates for different needs of enterprises and industries. Enterprises can simply use these templates to optimize their business processes with blockchain technology

ContractLand's Ecosystem

ContractLand is a decentralized ecosystem built upon community developers, enterprices, and users

Template Creation

A smart contract creation and sharing platform between developers and enterprises is created, effectively reducing the technical barriers for enterprises. Developers are incentivized by token rewards to participate in the creation of industry standard smart contract templates

Contract Deployment

Contract templates allow enterprises to quickly deploy and market business contracts directly through fool-proof templates, utilizing the traceability and immutability of blockchain. Enterprises can browse and choice the template that best fit their business needs, and deploy them using their own business specific parameters

Decentralized Exchange

Decentralized transaction markets between enterprises and enterprises, between enterprises and users allow enterprises to quickly deploy and market smart business contracts directly through fool-proof templates, utilize the traceability of blockchain, can not be tampered with, and execute contracts to third parties, etc. Characteristics, making the contract between enterprises is very quick and safe transfer

A Diversified Range of Templates

The decentralized template creation model enables an enriched coverage of contract templates for different industries

Tokens

Most businesses lack the technical and business expertise to create secure token contracts, often resulting in hacks and lost of tokens. ContractLand will use templates audited by security experts to reduce the cost and increase security of token creation

Token Sale

Token is one of the most prominent usecase of blockchain. At present, only token-manufacturing links operate with smart contract technology. Will put the entire pre-sale process all under the management of intelligent contracts, eliminating the risk of pre-sale process

Factoring Contract

The factoring industry mainly based on traditional paper contracts. A large amount of manpower is required to identify the authenticity of contracts, which is inefficient and has poor liquidity. ContractLand enables easy customization and creation of factoring contracts

Pre-Paid Services

Smart contracts eliminate chargeback, central billing system failure or Man-made destruction of the pre-paid industry. ContractLand enables the deployment of prepaid business contracts completely without barriers, and through the purse makes all the prepaid services more fluid

Event & Ticket Industry

Businesses can create a transparent ticketing ecosystem on the blockchain. This can create an effective secondary fare market and completely eliminate the existence of ticket fraud
